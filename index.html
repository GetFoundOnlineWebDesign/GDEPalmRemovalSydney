When Should You Remove a Palm Tree?

Palms are often removed or heavily cleaned when they start to cause problems such as:

  • Regularly dropping heavy fronds onto roofs, cars or footpaths
  • Seed pods creating constant mess on driveways, pools and outdoor areas
  • Palms outgrowing the space and leaning towards houses or powerlines
  • Roots causing damage to paving, retaining walls or underground services
  • Habitat for pests such as rats, ibis or bats
  • Insurance or safety concerns identified by property managers or body corporates

If you’re unsure whether a palm needs removal or can simply be cleaned and maintained, GDE’s Level 5 arborist can inspect the tree and provide practical recommendations.
